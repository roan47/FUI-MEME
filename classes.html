Our Classes

The Post

Ever wondered how muggles send letters?

In this class we will work to understand how muggles send written communication via a system called the Post. You will discovered how to use the post, the purpose of those boxes in front of muggle houses, what a stamp is, and who exactly the mail carrier is.

Travel

How do you travel without Portkeys or the Floo Network?

In this class we will teach you how to travel in the muggle world. You will learn what a passport is and why it is necessary. Additionally, we will provide instruction on navigating many forms of public transportation.

See classes
Automobiles

What are those things that muggles drive around in?

In this class we discuss muggle personal transportation. Many muggles own things called vehicles that they use to get themselves from place to place. You will understand why vehicles are so important in the muggle world.

See classes
School

How do muggle children learn? Do they have to take tests?

In this class you will learn all about the muggle education system. You will understand the different levels muggle children progress through. We will discuss the evaluations that muggles take and how they compare to O.W.L.'s.

See classes
Football

What sports do muggle's play? Quidditch?

In this class you will work to understand the most popular muggle sport, Football. We will discuss the rules of the games and the popular teams. You will learn what you need to do to fit in with muggle sports fans and how to participate in sporting events.

Lore

What are myths and what is make-believe?

In this class, you will discover the tales that make up muggle lore. We will also discover how muggles came to believe that the wizarding world is all make-believe. We will talk about lore from around the muggle world.
